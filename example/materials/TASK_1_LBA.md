# Task 1 — Draft Letter Before Action

**Points available:** 45
**Type:** Correspondence (not privileged — seen by the other side)

---

## Your Task

Draft a **Letter Before Action** on behalf of **Fairway Events Ltd** to **Barker & Co Solicitors**.

---

## What the LBA Must Include

### Protocol Requirements
- [ ] Identified as a Letter Before Action with a clear heading
- [ ] References the Practice Direction — Pre-Action Conduct and Protocols
- [ ] Properly addressed to Barker & Co and/or Luxe Marquees directly
- [ ] Addresses the delay in responding to Barker & Co's October 2025 LBA
- [ ] Lists documents relied upon
- [ ] Proposes ADR (mediation) with reference to costs consequences (*Halsey v Milton Keynes*)
- [ ] Sets a deadline for response — 14 days

### Defence
- [ ] Disputes the claim clearly and unambiguously
- [ ] Identifies the right of set-off — counterclaim exceeds the balance due
- [ ] Addresses the contractual interest claim
- [ ] Links the defence to specific contractual breaches

### Counterclaim — Heads of Loss
- [ ] **Liquidated damages** — Clause 5.1, £2,000 for one day's delay (17→18 July), references time of the essence
- [ ] **Replacement furniture** — Clauses 3.1/3.2, cost £12,500 + VAT, Clause 3.3 right to replacement
- [ ] **Replacement lighting** — same analysis, cost £3,800 + VAT
- [ ] **Staff overtime** — £2,400, explains necessity
- [ ] **Deposit recovery** — repudiatory breach / total failure of consideration argument, £14,400 + VAT, claimed alternatively as set-off
- [ ] **Loss of Harringtons contract** — identified as consequential loss, approximate value stated, noted as to be quantified, factual basis given
- [ ] **Summary table** of all heads of loss with totals
- [ ] **Breaches clearly identified** — late installation (Clause 2.2), non-conforming equipment (Clauses 3.1/3.2), defective installation

---

## Important

> **The LBA goes to the other side. Do NOT include privileged advice, strategy or your assessment of weaknesses. Keep the tone firm, factual and professional.**
