# FACILITY AGREEMENT (extract)

**Borrower:** Cobalt Energy Holdings Ltd (registered number 07654398) (the "**Borrower**")
**Original Lenders:** the Original Lenders listed in **Schedule 1**
**Agent:** Northbank Markets plc, London Branch (the "**Agent**")
**Security Agent:** Northbank Trustees Limited (the "**Security Agent**")
**Date:** 2025-08-14
**Facility:** £180,000,000 multi-currency revolving credit and term loan facility based on the **LMA Investment Grade Single Currency Term and Revolving Facilities Agreement**.

> *Extract of the key operative provisions. Schedules (mandatory costs, conditions precedent, repayment schedule, forms of utilisation request, security documents) omitted.*

## 1. THE FACILITIES

1.1 The Original Lenders make available to the Borrower:

   (a) **Facility A**: a sterling term loan facility in an aggregate amount of £120,000,000; and
   (b) **Facility B**: a multi-currency revolving credit facility in an aggregate amount of £60,000,000.

1.2 The proceeds of Facility A shall be applied (a) in refinancing the Existing Facilities and (b) for general corporate purposes of the Group. The proceeds of Facility B shall be applied for general corporate purposes and working capital of the Group.

## 2. INTEREST

2.1 The rate of interest on each Loan is the percentage rate per annum which is the aggregate of (a) the **Margin** and (b) **SONIA** (compounded daily) for the relevant Interest Period.

2.2 The Margin is:

   (a) **2.25%** per annum during the period from the Closing Date until the first Margin Adjustment Date; and
   (b) thereafter, the rate determined by reference to the **Net Leverage Ratio** as set out in the Margin Grid in Schedule 4 (ranging from 1.75% to 3.25%).

2.3 Default interest applies at **1% per annum above the rate** that would otherwise apply to an Overdue Amount.

## 3. REPAYMENT

3.1 Facility A shall be repaid in semi-annual instalments commencing 18 months after the Closing Date as set out in Schedule 5 (Amortisation), with a Final Repayment Date falling **6 years** after the Closing Date.

3.2 Facility B shall be repaid in full on the **Termination Date** of Facility B (5 years after the Closing Date), and each Utilisation under Facility B shall be repaid on the last day of its Interest Period.

3.3 Voluntary prepayment is permitted on 5 Business Days' notice in minimum amounts of £5,000,000. No prepayment fee applies.

## 4. MANDATORY PREPAYMENT

4.1 **Change of Control.** Upon the occurrence of a Change of Control:

   (a) the Borrower shall promptly notify the Agent;
   (b) no Lender shall be obliged to fund any Utilisation;
   (c) each Lender may, by notice to the Agent within 30 days of the Change of Control notice, cancel its Commitment and require prepayment of its participation in all outstanding Loans together with accrued interest and breakage costs.

4.2 **Asset Disposals.** Net Disposal Proceeds in excess of £10,000,000 in aggregate in any financial year shall be applied in mandatory prepayment, save to the extent reinvested in the business of the Group within 12 months.

4.3 **Excess Cashflow Sweep.** If the Net Leverage Ratio at the end of any financial year is greater than 3.0:1, **50% of Excess Cashflow** shall be applied in mandatory prepayment of Facility A within 90 days of delivery of the audited accounts.

## 5. REPRESENTATIONS

5.1 Each Obligor makes the Repetitive Representations on the date of this Agreement, on each Utilisation Date, and on the first day of each Interest Period (or, in the case of the Repeating Representations Light List, on each such date but without the qualifier in respect of no material litigation):

   (a) due incorporation and power and authority;
   (b) binding obligations;
   (c) no conflict with any law or other agreement;
   (d) validity and admissibility in evidence;
   (e) no Event of Default has occurred and is continuing;
   (f) no Material Adverse Change has occurred since the last Compliance Certificate;
   (g) the Information Memorandum is true and accurate in all material respects;
   (h) sanctions, anti-corruption and anti-money-laundering compliance.

## 6. INFORMATION AND COMPLIANCE

6.1 The Borrower shall deliver:

   (a) audited consolidated annual financial statements within 120 days of each financial year end;
   (b) unaudited quarterly management accounts within 60 days of each quarter end;
   (c) a Compliance Certificate with each set of financial statements signed by two directors;
   (d) annual budget at least 30 days before each financial year start.

## 7. FINANCIAL COVENANTS

7.1 The Borrower shall ensure that, on each Test Date:

   (a) **Net Leverage Ratio** shall not exceed **3.50:1** (stepping down to 3.25:1 from the third Test Date and to 3.00:1 from the fifth Test Date);
   (b) **Interest Cover** shall not be less than **4.00:1**;
   (c) **Capex** in any financial year shall not exceed the higher of £20,000,000 and 110% of budgeted capex (with an unused amount carry-forward of up to 50%).

7.2 **Equity Cure.** A breach of clause 7.1(a) or 7.1(b) may be cured by the receipt of New Shareholder Injection treated as a reduction in Total Net Debt (and, in the case of Interest Cover only, an increase in EBITDA). Cures are subject to a maximum of two in any 12-month period and four in the life of the Facilities, may not be in consecutive Test Periods, and shall be applied in mandatory prepayment.

## 8. GENERAL UNDERTAKINGS

8.1 The Group shall not, without the consent of the Majority Lenders:

   (a) create any Security other than Permitted Security;
   (b) make any Loans other than Permitted Loans;
   (c) dispose of any asset save Permitted Disposals;
   (d) acquire any company or business save Permitted Acquisitions (capped at £25,000,000 per acquisition and £75,000,000 in aggregate);
   (e) change the general nature of the business of the Group;
   (f) declare any Restricted Payment whilst a Default is continuing.

## 9. EVENTS OF DEFAULT

9.1 Each of the following is an Event of Default:

   (a) **Non-payment.** Failure to pay any amount when due (with a 3 Business Day grace period for administrative/technical failure only).
   (b) **Financial covenants.** Breach of clause 7.1 (subject to the Equity Cure rights).
   (c) **Other obligations.** Failure to perform any other obligation under the Finance Documents, not remedied within 20 Business Days.
   (d) **Misrepresentation.** Any representation made by an Obligor is materially incorrect.
   (e) **Cross-default.** Financial Indebtedness of any Group Company in excess of £5,000,000 becomes due or capable of being declared due before its scheduled maturity.
   (f) **Insolvency** of any Obligor or Material Subsidiary.
   (g) **Insolvency proceedings.**
   (h) **Creditors' process** affecting assets with a value exceeding £2,500,000.
   (i) **Cessation of business.**
   (j) **Audit qualification** that is materially adverse.
   (k) **Repudiation** of any Finance Document.
   (l) **Material Adverse Change.**

9.2 On an Event of Default which is continuing, the Agent may (and shall on instruction of the Majority Lenders): cancel the Total Commitments, declare all amounts immediately due and payable, and enforce the Transaction Security.

## 10. SECURITY

10.1 The obligations under this Agreement are secured by the **Transaction Security** granted by each Obligor in favour of the Security Agent on behalf of the Secured Parties, comprising:

   (a) English-law debenture (fixed and floating charges over substantially all assets);
   (b) share charges over each Material Subsidiary;
   (c) Scots-law and Norwegian-law security in respect of overseas assets (set out in Schedule 7);
   (d) account control agreements.

## 11. GOVERNING LAW

11.1 This Agreement is governed by the law of England and Wales. The courts of England and Wales have exclusive jurisdiction.
