# MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

**THIS AGREEMENT** is made on **2026-04-18**

**BETWEEN:**

**(1) NORTHWIND INDUSTRIES LIMITED**, a company incorporated in England and Wales (registered number 02145671) whose registered office is at 14 Lichfield Road, Birmingham B6 5SU ("**Northwind**"); and

**(2) PROSPECTIVE PARTY** (as defined in the Schedule).

**BACKGROUND**

(A) The parties wish to discuss a possible transaction involving the sale of all or part of the issued share capital of Northwind (the "**Permitted Purpose**").

(B) In connection with the Permitted Purpose, each party may disclose Confidential Information to the other.

(C) The parties wish to record their agreement on the basis on which Confidential Information will be disclosed and used.

## 1. DEFINITIONS

1.1 In this Agreement:

"**Confidential Information**" means any information disclosed by a party (the "**Disclosing Party**") to the other party (the "**Receiving Party**") in connection with the Permitted Purpose, in any form, whether oral, written, electronic or otherwise, that is identified as confidential at the time of disclosure or that a reasonable person would understand to be confidential, including without limitation:

   (a) the existence and terms of the parties' discussions;
   (b) the financial information, business plans, customer information, supplier information and pricing information of the Disclosing Party;
   (c) any technical, operational or commercial information relating to the Disclosing Party's business;

but does not include information that:

   (i) is or becomes publicly available without breach of this Agreement;
   (ii) was lawfully known to the Receiving Party prior to disclosure;
   (iii) is independently developed by the Receiving Party without reference to the disclosed information; or
   (iv) is rightfully obtained from a third party without breach of any duty of confidentiality.

For the avoidance of doubt, no information shall be excluded by reason only that it can be reverse-engineered from publicly available materials.

"**Representatives**" means, in relation to each party, its directors, officers, employees, professional advisers and (in the case of the Receiving Party only) any potential financiers approved in writing by the Disclosing Party.

## 2. CONFIDENTIALITY OBLIGATIONS

2.1 The Receiving Party shall:

   (a) keep the Confidential Information strictly confidential;
   (b) use the Confidential Information only for the Permitted Purpose;
   (c) not disclose the Confidential Information to any person other than its Representatives who have a need to know for the Permitted Purpose;
   (d) ensure that each Representative to whom Confidential Information is disclosed is bound by confidentiality obligations no less stringent than those in this Agreement;
   (e) be responsible for any breach of this Agreement by any of its Representatives;
   (f) take all reasonable security precautions to protect the Confidential Information from unauthorised access, use or disclosure.

## 3. AI AND AUTOMATED PROCESSING

3.1 The Receiving Party shall not input any Confidential Information into any third-party artificial intelligence, machine-learning or large-language-model system except:

   (a) services explicitly approved by the Disclosing Party in writing; or
   (b) services where the Receiving Party has obtained a binding written commitment from the provider that inputs will not be used for model training and will be processed in a closed-tenant environment with appropriate security controls.

3.2 Any breach of clause 3.1 shall constitute a material breach of this Agreement.

## 4. RETURN OR DESTRUCTION

4.1 On the earlier of (a) the conclusion of the Permitted Purpose; or (b) written request by the Disclosing Party, the Receiving Party shall within 30 days return or, at the Disclosing Party's option, destroy all Confidential Information in its possession, including all copies, extracts and derivatives.

4.2 The Receiving Party may retain (i) one copy of the Confidential Information solely for legal, regulatory or audit compliance purposes; and (ii) automatic system backups, in each case for so long as the obligations of confidentiality in this Agreement subsist.

## 5. NO LICENCE OR REPRESENTATION

5.1 Nothing in this Agreement transfers any intellectual property rights or grants any licence in respect of the Confidential Information, save the limited right to use Confidential Information for the Permitted Purpose.

5.2 The Disclosing Party makes no representation or warranty as to the accuracy or completeness of any Confidential Information disclosed under this Agreement.

## 6. TERM

6.1 This Agreement shall continue in force for a period of two (2) years from the date of this Agreement.

## 7. GENERAL

7.1 **Remedies**. The parties acknowledge that damages may not be an adequate remedy for breach of this Agreement and that injunctive relief may be appropriate.

7.2 **Notices**. Notices shall be in writing and delivered by hand or by email to the addresses set out in the Schedule.

7.3 **Entire agreement**. This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties relating to its subject matter.

7.4 **Governing law and jurisdiction**. This Agreement and any dispute or claim (including non-contractual disputes or claims) arising out of or in connection with it shall be governed by, and construed in accordance with, the law of England and Wales. The parties irrevocably agree that the courts of England and Wales shall have exclusive jurisdiction.
