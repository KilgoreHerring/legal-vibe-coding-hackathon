# COMMERCIAL LEASE - SUMMARY OF KEY TERMS

**Property:** Unit 1A, 218 Westgate Place, London W6 9PN
**Landlord:** Westgate Property Partners LLP
**Tenant:** Specimen Coffee Co. Limited
**Date of lease:** 2024-03-22

> *This is a working summary of the key commercial terms only. The full lease runs to 84 pages including schedules.*

## 1. TERM

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| Commencement date | 1 April 2024 |
| Term | 10 years |
| Expiry date | 31 March 2034 |
| 1954 Act | The lease is **inside the Act** (no exclusion notice served). Tenant has security of tenure. |

## 2. RENT

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| Initial rent | £148,000 per annum, exclusive of VAT |
| Payment | Quarterly in advance on usual quarter days |
| Rent reviews | Every 5 years (1 April 2029 and 1 April 2034 if renewed) |
| Review mechanism | Open-market upwards-only review, with 4% per annum cap and 0% collar |
| VAT | Landlord has elected to waive exemption; rent and other sums subject to VAT |

## 3. SERVICE CHARGE AND OTHER OUTGOINGS

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| Service charge | Variable; estimate £18,200 per annum (year 1) |
| Cap | 5% above the previous year's actual service charge each year |
| Insurance rent | Landlord insures, recharges to tenant at cost |
| Business rates | Tenant's responsibility |
| Utilities | Tenant's responsibility |

## 4. BREAK CLAUSE

The tenant may terminate the lease on the **fifth anniversary** (1 April 2029) by giving the landlord **not less than 9 months' prior written notice**.

The break is conditional only on:

(a) payment of all rents up to (but not beyond) the break date; and
(b) giving back vacant possession (free of underleases and licences).

No further conditions apply (no requirement to make good, no compliance prerequisite). Note: this is an **unusually tenant-friendly** break.

## 5. ALIENATION

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| Assignment | Permitted with landlord's prior written consent (not to be unreasonably withheld or delayed). AGA may be required only in circumstances permitted by section 19(1A) of the Landlord and Tenant Act 1927. |
| Underletting | Permitted of the whole only, on similar terms to this lease. Sub-rent must equal market rent. Underletting requires landlord's prior written consent. |
| Sharing with group companies | Permitted on notice |
| Charging | Permitted with landlord's consent |

## 6. ALTERATIONS

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| Structural alterations | Prohibited |
| Internal non-structural | Permitted with landlord's consent (not to be unreasonably withheld) |
| External signage | Permitted with consent, subject to landlord's house style |
| Reinstatement | At end of term, landlord may require reinstatement of tenant alterations to original condition |

## 7. PERMITTED USE

The premises may be used only as a **café and coffee retail unit** within Class E of the Town and Country Planning (Use Classes) Order 1987 (as amended).

## 8. REPAIR AND DECORATION

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| Repairing obligation | Tenant on full repairing and insuring (FRI) basis |
| Schedule of condition | Yes - photographic schedule annexed at Schedule 4. Tenant's obligation is to keep the premises in no worse condition than as at the schedule. |
| Internal redecoration | Every 5 years and in the last 6 months of the term |
| Compliance with legislation | Tenant's responsibility |

## 9. INSURANCE

The landlord insures the building for full reinstatement value, third-party liability and 3 years' loss of rent. Tenant pays insurance rent at cost. Tenant insures own contents and tenant fit-out.

## 10. RENT DEPOSIT

Six months' rent (£74,000 + VAT) held in a rent deposit account. Drawable in case of arrears. Released at end of term subject to compliance with covenants.

## 11. GUARANTOR

Specimen Group Holdings Limited (parent of tenant) is the guarantor.

## 12. GOVERNING LAW

The lease is governed by the law of England and Wales.

---

## RISK NOTES (INTERNAL)

- **Break clause** is exceptionally tenant-friendly. Landlord cannot rely on technical conditions to prevent the break.
- **Rent review cap of 4% pa** is favourable to tenant in current inflationary environment.
- **No keep-open clause** - tenant can go dark for the full term.
- **Schedule of condition** materially limits tenant's repair obligation - check landlord's exit strategy.
- **AGA requirement** is appropriately limited to section 19(1A) circumstances; not an open-ended requirement.
