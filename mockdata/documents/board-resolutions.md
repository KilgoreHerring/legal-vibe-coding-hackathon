# WRITTEN RESOLUTIONS OF THE DIRECTORS

## HELIX THERAPEUTICS PLC

**Company number:** 09147264
**Date:** 2026-05-11
**Form:** Written resolutions of the directors of the Company in accordance with the Articles

---

The undersigned, being all of the directors of Helix Therapeutics plc (the "**Company**"), hereby record the following resolutions adopted in accordance with the Company's articles of association.

## 1. CONFLICTS OF INTEREST

The chair noted that no director declared any interest in any matter to be considered.

## 2. SERIES C INVESTMENT - APPROVAL OF TERMS

**IT WAS RESOLVED** that:

2.1 the issue of up to **18,500,000 new Series C Preference Shares** of £0.0001 each in the capital of the Company at a price of $10.00 per share (gross proceeds approximately $185,000,000) on the terms set out in the Subscription and Shareholders' Agreement in agreed form (the "**Series C SSA**"), be and is approved;

2.2 the form and terms of the Series C SSA, the Articles of Association amendments, the disclosure letter and all related transaction documents (the "**Transaction Documents**") be and are approved;

2.3 any **two directors** be and are authorised to sign and execute the Transaction Documents on behalf of the Company, with such amendments as such directors may consider appropriate;

2.4 the chair (or any director) be and is authorised to take such further actions as are reasonably necessary or desirable to give effect to these resolutions, including making any required filings at Companies House.

## 3. AMENDMENT TO ARTICLES OF ASSOCIATION

**IT WAS RESOLVED** that, conditional upon the passing by the shareholders of a special resolution to that effect:

3.1 the existing Articles of Association of the Company be and are replaced in their entirety with the new Articles in agreed form (the "**New Articles**");

3.2 the New Articles incorporate the new rights of the Series C Preference Shares as set out in the Series C SSA, including:

   (a) liquidation preference: 1x non-participating;
   (b) anti-dilution: broad-based weighted average;
   (c) protective provisions in respect of the matters listed in Schedule 5 of the New Articles;
   (d) information rights consistent with US growth-stage practice;
   (e) registration rights consistent with the existing Series A and B rights, modified as set out in Schedule 6.

## 4. EMPLOYEE OPTION POOL REFRESH

**IT WAS RESOLVED** that:

4.1 the unallocated portion of the existing employee share option pool be and is increased by issuing such additional ordinary shares to the EBT trustee as bring the unallocated pool to **15% of the fully diluted post-money capital**, conditional on completion of the Series C investment;

4.2 the rules of the **Helix 2026 EMI Scheme** in agreed form be and are adopted;

4.3 awards under the 2026 EMI Scheme to the persons and on the terms set out in Schedule 8 be and are approved.

## 5. INVESTOR DIRECTOR APPOINTMENT

**IT WAS RESOLVED** that, conditional on completion of the Series C investment:

5.1 **Anjali Patel** (nominee of Atlas Crossover Partners) be and is appointed as a non-executive director of the Company with effect from the Completion Date;

5.2 **Devorah Reinholt** (existing nominee of Series B lead investor) shall continue to serve as non-executive director and shall not stand down in connection with the Series C investment;

5.3 the chair be authorised to enter into a letter of appointment with Anjali Patel on the Company's standard terms.

## 6. BANKING AUTHORITIES

**IT WAS RESOLVED** that the existing banking authorities and signatory list be and are confirmed and shall continue in effect.

## 7. INSURANCE

**IT WAS RESOLVED** that the directors note the renewal of the directors' and officers' insurance with effect from the Completion Date with cover increased to £25,000,000 in aggregate.

## 8. CONFIRMATION

The undersigned confirm that they have read, understood and agree to be bound by these resolutions.
