# SETTLEMENT AGREEMENT

**THIS AGREEMENT** is made on **2026-02-19**

**BETWEEN:**

**(1) ALBION RETAIL HOLDINGS PLC** (registered number 04217836) of 8 Cavendish Square, London W1G 0PH (the "**Company**"); and

**(2) DEBORAH H. PRENTISS** of [address on file] (the "**Employee**").

**BACKGROUND**

(A) The Employee was employed by the Company under a service agreement dated 4 March 2019 (the "**Service Agreement**").

(B) The Employee's employment terminated on **31 January 2026** (the "**Termination Date**").

(C) The parties have entered into this Agreement to settle on a full and final basis any and all claims that the Employee has or may have against the Company arising out of the Employee's employment or its termination.

## 1. TERMINATION

1.1 The Employee's employment terminated on the Termination Date by reason of mutual agreement.

1.2 The Employee resigned as a director of the Company and any Group Company with effect from the Termination Date.

1.3 The Employee acknowledges that they will receive no further sums (and have no further entitlements) under the Service Agreement save as expressly set out in this Agreement.

## 2. PAYMENTS

2.1 Subject to clause 3 (Adviser's Certificate) and the Employee complying with their obligations under this Agreement, the Company shall pay the Employee:

   (a) **£28,400** in respect of accrued but untaken holiday, less PAYE deductions;
   (b) **£155,000** in lieu of the Employee's six-month notice period, less PAYE deductions (the "**PILON**");
   (c) **£275,000** as compensation for loss of office (the "**Compensation Payment**"), the first £30,000 of which shall be paid free of tax under section 403 of ITEPA 2003;
   (d) **£200,000** as a bonus payment in respect of the year ended 30 January 2026 (under the discretionary bonus scheme), less PAYE deductions;
   (e) up to **£10,000** plus VAT as a contribution towards the Employee's outplacement support, paid directly to the provider on receipt of invoices;
   (f) up to **£3,500** plus VAT as a contribution towards the Employee's legal costs in connection with this Agreement, paid directly to the Employee's solicitors.

## 3. ADVISER'S CERTIFICATE

3.1 The Employee confirms that they have received independent legal advice from a relevant independent adviser as defined in section 203(3A) of the Employment Rights Act 1996 (a "**Relevant Independent Adviser**") regarding the terms and effect of this Agreement and in particular its effect on the Employee's ability to bring statutory claims against the Company.

3.2 The Employee's Relevant Independent Adviser shall sign the Adviser's Certificate at Schedule 1.

## 4. WAIVER OF CLAIMS

4.1 The Employee accepts the payments and benefits set out in clause 2 in full and final settlement of all claims against the Company and any Group Company, whether known or unknown, suspected or unsuspected, contemplated or otherwise, arising out of or in connection with the Employee's employment or its termination, including (without limitation) the specific statutory claims listed in **Schedule 2** (which contains the specific statutory claims required for the waiver to be effective under the relevant statutes), save for:

   (a) any claim in respect of personal injury of which the Employee is not aware and could not reasonably be aware as at the date of this Agreement;
   (b) any claim in respect of accrued pension entitlements; and
   (c) any claim to enforce the terms of this Agreement.

## 5. CONTINUING OBLIGATIONS

5.1 The post-termination restrictions in clauses 18 to 22 of the Service Agreement shall continue to apply, save that the duration of the non-competition restriction is reduced from 12 months to **9 months**.

5.2 The Employee's obligations of confidentiality under clauses 16 and 17 of the Service Agreement shall continue indefinitely.

5.3 The Employee shall not, save as required by law, make any public statement (including on social media) regarding the Company, any Group Company or any of their officers or employees.

## 6. AGREED ANNOUNCEMENT AND REFERENCE

6.1 The parties have agreed the form of internal and external announcement at **Schedule 3**. Neither party shall make any other announcement.

6.2 The Company shall, on request and within 14 days, provide a reference in the form set out in **Schedule 4**.

## 7. RETURN OF PROPERTY

7.1 The Employee confirms that they have, on or before the Termination Date, returned all Company property and Confidential Information.

## 8. WARRANTIES BY THE EMPLOYEE

8.1 The Employee warrants that:

   (a) they are not aware of any matter that, if disclosed, would or might constitute or lead to a claim against the Company;
   (b) they have not commenced any proceedings against the Company;
   (c) the Adviser's Certificate is signed by a Relevant Independent Adviser holding the relevant insurance.

8.2 If any warranty in clause 8.1 is or becomes untrue, the Company shall be entitled to recover the Compensation Payment as a debt.

## 9. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

9.1 This Agreement (and the Service Agreement to the extent its terms are continued) constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.

## 10. GOVERNING LAW

10.1 This Agreement shall be governed by the law of England and Wales. The courts of England and Wales shall have exclusive jurisdiction.
