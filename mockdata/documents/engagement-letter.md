Dear Anne,

Thank you for instructing us in connection with **Project Birch**, the proposed acquisition by [Portfolio Company] of [the Target] (the "**Transaction**"). I am writing to confirm the basis on which we will act.

## 1. SCOPE OF WORK

We are instructed to act as English legal counsel to Tessera Capital Partners (and its acquiring portfolio company) in connection with the Transaction, including:

(a) Phase 0 intelligence work in respect of the Target;
(b) legal due diligence in the disclosed scope (Schedule 1);
(c) drafting and negotiation of the share purchase agreement and ancillary documents;
(d) advising on warranty and indemnity insurance, where applicable;
(e) signing and completion mechanics, including filings.

We will not, unless separately instructed, advise on:

(i) tax structuring beyond standard transaction tax inputs;
(ii) competition or merger control analysis (referred to Bramble & Finch LLP, separately engaged);
(iii) regulatory consents requiring sector-specialist advice;
(iv) employment due diligence beyond a high-level review of the Target's senior team.

## 2. TEAM

The lead partner is **Catherine Whitfield**. The day-to-day matter manager is **Sarah Lin** (Associate). **Marcus Holloway** (Trainee) and **Vikram Chen** (Associate, Tax) will provide additional support. Specialist input from other practice groups will be drawn in as needed.
