# PRIVACY NOTICE - LATTICE NETWORKS

**Last updated:** 2026-04-22

This Privacy Notice explains how Lattice Networks Ltd ("**Lattice**", "**we**", "**us**") collects, uses and shares personal data when you use our website, our customer-facing services, or otherwise interact with us. We are the controller in respect of the personal data described in this notice.

## 1. WHO WE ARE

Lattice Networks Ltd is a company registered in England and Wales (company number 12918274) with registered office at 1 Atlantic Tower, 11 King Street, Manchester M2 6AY.

Our Data Protection Officer can be contacted at **dpo@lattice-networks.example**.

## 2. PERSONAL DATA WE COLLECT

We collect the following categories of personal data:

| Category | Examples | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Identity data | Name, job title, employer | You; LinkedIn (where you have made it public) |
| Contact data | Business email, phone, postal address | You |
| Account data | Username, password (hashed), preferences | You; created on sign-up |
| Usage data | IP address, device, browser, pages visited, click events | Automatically collected |
| Communications | Records of correspondence with us | You; us |
| Marketing data | Preferences for receiving marketing | You; opt-in/opt-out actions |

We do not knowingly collect special category data. If you provide us with such data inadvertently, please ask us to delete it.

## 3. HOW WE USE PERSONAL DATA AND OUR LEGAL BASIS

| Purpose | Legal basis |
|---|---|
| Providing the services you have signed up for | Contract |
| Account management and customer support | Contract; legitimate interests |
| Securing our network and preventing fraud | Legitimate interests; legal obligation |
| Direct marketing to existing customers (similar products) | Legitimate interests (with right to opt out) |
| Direct marketing to prospects | Consent |
| Compliance with law (regulatory, tax, accounting) | Legal obligation |
| Improving our products through aggregated analytics | Legitimate interests |

## 4. SHARING

We share personal data with:

(a) **Sub-processors** that provide infrastructure and tooling for the services. A current list is at https://lattice-networks.example/subprocessors.

(b) **Professional advisers** (lawyers, accountants, auditors) where necessary.

(c) **Authorities** where required by law or court order.

(d) **Acquirers** in the event of a corporate transaction (under appropriate confidentiality obligations).

We do **not** sell personal data.

## 5. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS

Some of our sub-processors are located outside the UK, including in the United States, Ireland, Germany and India. Where we transfer personal data outside the UK, we use:

(a) the UK International Data Transfer Addendum;
(b) the EU Standard Contractual Clauses (where the recipient is in the EU/EEA, no additional measure is needed);
(c) other approved transfer mechanisms; and

we conduct and document a transfer impact assessment for each transfer to a country not subject to a UK adequacy decision.

## 6. RETENTION

We retain personal data for the periods set out in our retention schedule, which is broadly:

| Data | Retention period |
|---|---|
| Account data | Duration of the contract + 6 years |
| Communications | 6 years from last contact |
| Usage logs | 12 months (security logs); 36 months (commercial analytics, aggregated) |
| Marketing data | Until consent withdrawn or 36 months of inactivity |
| Tax records | 6 years from end of relevant tax year |

After the retention period, personal data is securely deleted or irreversibly anonymised.

## 7. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

(a) be informed about the processing of your personal data (this notice);
(b) access your personal data (a "subject access request" or "DSAR");
(c) rectify inaccurate personal data;
(d) erase personal data in certain circumstances ("right to be forgotten");
(e) restrict processing;
(f) object to processing (including direct marketing);
(g) data portability;
(h) not be subject to solely automated decisions with legal or significant effect;
(i) withdraw consent where processing is based on consent;
(j) lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at **ico.org.uk** or call **0303 123 1113**.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **privacy@lattice-networks.example**.

## 8. COOKIES

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies. The full Cookie Notice is available at https://lattice-networks.example/cookies.

Strictly necessary cookies are set by default. Other cookies (analytics, marketing) are set only with your consent, which you can manage at any time via the cookie banner. We have separated "Accept all" and "Reject all" controls to give equal prominence to both choices.

## 9. AUTOMATED DECISION MAKING

We do not use solely automated decision-making with legal or similarly significant effect on you.

We do use AI-powered features within our products. These features process customer data only on the customer's instructions, do not train on customer data, and operate in single-tenant environments. Customers can disable AI features via the platform settings.

## 10. SECURITY

We maintain technical and organisational measures appropriate to the risk, including:

- TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 at rest;
- ISO/IEC 27001:2022 certification;
- annual penetration testing;
- segregated test/staging/production environments;
- least-privilege access controls and quarterly access reviews.
