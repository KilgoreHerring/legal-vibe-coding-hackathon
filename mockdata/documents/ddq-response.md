## SECTION F - DATA PROTECTION

### F.1 Personal data processing

**Target response:** The Company is a controller in respect of customer account data and a processor in respect of customer end-user data processed on the customer's behalf. The full register of processing activities is at Annex F-1.

### F.2 Data protection compliance

**Target response:** The Company has:

- a Privacy Notice published at https://verdant-dataops.example/privacy (last updated 2025-11-08);
- a Cookie Notice with separate "accept all" and "reject all" controls of equal prominence;
- DPAs with all sub-processors (Annex F-2);
- an internal data breach procedure;
- annual mandatory data protection training for staff.

### F.3 Personal data breaches

**Target response:** The Company has reported one personal data breach to the ICO in the last 36 months (May 2025), arising from a misconfigured S3 bucket containing logs. No regulatory action followed. Internal post-incident review at Annex F-3.

> *Buyer follow-up: please provide the May 2025 incident report and the ICO correspondence in full.*

### F.4 International transfers

**Target response:** Personal data is transferred to the United States (sub-processor: cloud hosting; sub-processor: analytics) and to India (sub-processor: customer support). UK IDTA and EU SCCs are in place. Transfer impact assessments are at Annex F-5 (last refresh 2025-09-12).

> *Buyer follow-up: please refresh the transfer impact assessments to incorporate the September 2025 ICO guidance and the more recent CJEU developments.*
