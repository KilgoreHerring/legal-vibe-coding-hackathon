# SHAREHOLDERS' AGREEMENT (extract)

**Company:** Helix Therapeutics plc (registered number 09112846) (the "**Company**")
**Date:** 2026-05-09
**Round:** Series C

**PARTIES:**

**(1)** the **FOUNDERS** listed in Schedule 1;
**(2)** the **EXISTING INVESTORS** listed in Schedule 2;
**(3) MERIDIAN GROWTH PARTNERS V SCSp** (Luxembourg) (the "**Lead Investor**") and the other **NEW INVESTORS** listed in Schedule 3;
**(4)** the **COMPANY**.

> *Extract of the operative provisions. Schedules (cap table, reserved matters list, deed of adherence, leaver matrix) omitted.*

## 1. INTERPRETATION

1.1 "**Series C Shares**" means the Series C Preferred Shares of £0.0001 each issued pursuant to the Subscription Agreement dated on or about the date of this Agreement.

1.2 "**Investor Majority**" means the holders of more than 66.7% of the Preferred Shares in issue from time to time (acting together as a single class).

1.3 "**Bad Leaver**" means a Founder who ceases to be employed by or provide services to any Group Company by reason of (a) summary dismissal for cause, (b) voluntary resignation prior to the third anniversary of Completion or (c) material breach of the Service Agreement or of clauses 14 (Restrictive Covenants) or 15 (Confidentiality).

1.4 "**Good Leaver**" means any Founder who ceases such employment or engagement for any reason other than as a Bad Leaver, including death, long-term incapacity, retirement at normal retirement age, and termination by the Company without cause.

## 2. BOARD COMPOSITION

2.1 The Board shall consist of up to **seven** directors:

   (a) up to two directors nominated by the Founders (the "**Founder Directors**");
   (b) one director nominated by the Lead Investor for so long as it holds not less than 5% of the issued share capital (the "**Lead Investor Director**");
   (c) one director nominated by the Existing Investors holding a majority of the Series A Shares;
   (d) the Chief Executive Officer for the time being;
   (e) up to two **Independent Non-Executive Directors** appointed with Investor Majority consent.

2.2 Quorum shall be three directors and must include the Lead Investor Director (or an alternate) for so long as a Lead Investor Director is entitled to be appointed.

## 3. RESERVED MATTERS

3.1 No Group Company shall take any of the matters listed in **Schedule 4 (Reserved Matters)** without the prior written consent of the Investor Majority. These include, in summary:

   (a) any amendment to the Articles or to this Agreement;
   (b) the issue, allotment, redemption or buy-back of any shares or securities (other than under the Approved Option Pool);
   (c) any merger, acquisition, disposal of a material asset or change to the principal business;
   (d) incurrence of borrowings or guarantees exceeding £2,000,000 in aggregate;
   (e) entry into any related-party transaction with a Founder, employee or their connected persons;
   (f) approval of the annual budget and any deviation of more than 15% from approved budget line items;
   (g) commencement or settlement of any litigation with a claim value exceeding £500,000;
   (h) any IPO, Trade Sale or other Exit Event;
   (i) the hire or dismissal of any C-suite executive;
   (j) declaration of any dividend.

## 4. TRANSFER RESTRICTIONS

4.1 No Shareholder may Transfer any Shares except (a) in accordance with this clause 4, (b) to a Permitted Transferee under clause 4.6, or (c) with Investor Majority consent.

4.2 **Right of First Refusal.** A Selling Shareholder shall first offer the Sale Shares to the other Shareholders pro rata to their holdings of the same class, by Transfer Notice setting out the price and material terms. The offer is open for 30 Business Days.

4.3 **Co-Sale Right.** If a Founder proposes to Transfer any Shares to a third-party purchaser, each Investor may elect to participate in the sale on the same terms pro rata.

## 5. DRAG-ALONG

5.1 If holders of more than **75% of the issued share capital** (including the Investor Majority) approve a sale of the Company on bona fide arm's-length terms to a third party (a "**Drag Sale**"), all other Shareholders shall be required to sell their Shares on the same terms, save that:

   (a) no Shareholder shall be required to give warranties beyond title, capacity and authority to sell;
   (b) no Shareholder's liability shall exceed the consideration actually received by that Shareholder; and
   (c) the consideration shall be distributed in accordance with the Liquidation Preference in the Articles.

## 6. TAG-ALONG

6.1 No Transfer of Shares which would result in a Change of Control shall be permitted unless the proposed purchaser has first offered to acquire all Shares of all Shareholders on the same terms.

## 7. ANTI-DILUTION

7.1 If the Company issues Shares at a price per share less than the Series C Issue Price (a "**Down Round**"), the Series C Conversion Price shall be adjusted in accordance with the **broad-based weighted-average** formula set out in the Articles.

## 8. INFORMATION RIGHTS

8.1 The Company shall provide to each Investor holding not less than 3% of the issued share capital:

   (a) annual audited accounts within four months of the financial year end;
   (b) unaudited management accounts within 30 days of each month end;
   (c) the annual budget at least 30 days before the start of each financial year;
   (d) such other information as a Major Investor may reasonably request.

## 9. PRE-EMPTION ON NEW ISSUES

9.1 Save in respect of (a) the Approved Option Pool, (b) shares issued in connection with a Drag Sale or IPO, or (c) shares issued as part of a bona fide commercial transaction with Investor Majority consent, no new shares shall be issued without first being offered pro rata to existing Shareholders.

## 10. LEAVER PROVISIONS

10.1 On a Founder becoming a **Bad Leaver**, that Founder's Shares shall be transferred to the Company (or its nominee) at the lower of (a) the price paid and (b) the nominal value.

10.2 On a Founder becoming a **Good Leaver**, that Founder's Vested Shares shall be retained and Unvested Shares shall be transferred at Fair Value.

10.3 Vesting: 25% on the first anniversary of Completion, then monthly in equal instalments over a further 36 months. **Acceleration:** full vesting on a Drag Sale or termination without cause within 12 months of a Change of Control.

## 11. NON-COMPETE

11.1 No Founder shall, for the period during which they hold Shares and for **24 months** thereafter, be engaged in any business which is in competition with the Group anywhere the Group carries on business.

## 12. EXIT

12.1 The Company and the Shareholders shall use reasonable endeavours to achieve an Exit Event (IPO or Trade Sale) within five years of Completion.

12.2 If no Exit Event has occurred by the seventh anniversary, the Investor Majority may instruct the Board to appoint advisers and run a sale process.

## 13. CHANGE OF CONTROL OF AN INVESTOR

13.1 If an Investor undergoes a Change of Control to a Competitor (as defined in Schedule 5), the Company may require the affected Investor to Transfer its Shares to the Founders or the other Investors at Fair Value.

## 14. CONFIDENTIALITY

14.1 Each party shall keep confidential the terms of this Agreement and any information received in its capacity as a Shareholder or Director, save as required by law or in connection with bona fide Permitted Transferees.

## 15. TERMINATION

15.1 This Agreement terminates in respect of any Shareholder when that Shareholder ceases to hold Shares, save for the surviving obligations in clauses 14 (Confidentiality), 11 (Non-Compete) and 16 (Governing Law).

## 16. GOVERNING LAW

16.1 This Agreement is governed by the law of England and Wales. The courts of England and Wales have exclusive jurisdiction.
